# License Plate Pre-sale Voucher FAQ

## Voucher Redemption & Fees

- Redeeming a voucher **requires a license plate replacement fee of $36.90**, as mandated by Florida laws, in addition to the previously paid voucher fee.
- If a motorist has a **two-year registration**, the fee may be **$66.90**, depending on the remaining duration.
- If registration renewal occurs **at the same time as voucher redemption**, the replacement fee will be added to the renewal.
- Motorists must bring **proof of Florida insurance** when redeeming their voucher.
- If a motorist **does not have a voucher**, the total fee will be **$30 specialty plate fee + $36.90 replacement fee = $66.90.**
- Depending on the transaction, the customer is responsible for all other applicable fees.

🔹 **Source of Information:** [License Plate Voucher Redemption - Tax Collector of Escambia County](https://escambiataxcollector.com/license-plate-voucher-redemption/)

## What is a Voucher?

Upon the Florida Legislature’s approval of any new specialty license plate, the Florida Department of Highway Safety and Motor Vehicles (FLHSMV) **requires the sale of 3,000 pre-sale vouchers** before the new plate can be produced and distributed.

- Once **3,000 vouchers are sold**, license plates are printed and delivered to Florida Tax Collector Offices.
- **Production time:** **4-5 months** after reaching the sales threshold.
- Customers have **one year** to redeem their voucher or purchase a plate outright.
- **Who can buy the plate?**
    - **Vouchers:** Any person with a voucher can purchase the plate.
    - **No voucher?** You can still buy the plate outright.
- **Annual renewals:**
    - The plate must maintain **3,000 active registrations per year** to stay in production.
    - If the plate falls below 3,000, it enters a **one-year probation period** and may be discontinued.
    - If renewals exceed 3,000 during probation, the plate remains in circulation.
    - **January 1st law.** Every January 1st the department will discontinue the license plate with the lowest number of valid registration (this could happen if the valid registrations are above 3,000)

## Redeeming Pre-Sale Vouchers

**I purchased a pre-sale voucher. Can I redeem it for a license plate?**

**Yes.** A pre-sale voucher is assigned to a person. Once redeemed, the license plate is assigned to a **vehicle** and cannot be kept as a souvenir.

- Some supporters purchased vouchers **only to support Hatzalah South Florida Emergency Medical Services (HSF EMS)** without intending to display the plate.
- HSF EMS received **$75,000 from pre-sale vouchers** to meet the 3,000-pre-sale requirement.
- Some customers may never redeem their voucher, but their purchase still contributed to launching the plate.

**I ordered a plate. Can I keep it as a souvenir?**

**No.** Once redeemed, the license plate must be assigned to a vehicle. You must replace your current plate with the new one.

## Pre-Sale Period & Refunds

**Is the pre-sale time period for vouchers still open?**

❌ **No.** The pre-sale period ended on **October 14, 2024**, after reaching 3,000 pre-sales.

**Are pre-sale vouchers refundable or transferable?**

❌ **No.** Once the pre-sale requirement was met, **refunds and transfers are not available.**

Presale vouchers are no longer needed, but someone can purchase a Gift Certificate. The vouchers and gift certificates are non-transferable.

## Additional Fees & Ordering

**Will there be additional fees when the plate is manufactured?**

✅ **Most likely.** The voucher **covers the first year’s specialty plate fee** once available at tax collector offices.
✅ **If your plate is not due for a mandatory replacement (every 10 years)**, you may need to pay **$28.00 - $36.90** to replace it.
✅ **These fees do not cover annual registration renewals.**

**Is the order link for Escambia residents only?**

❌ **No.** Any Florida motorist can use the site.
✅ If gifting a voucher, the recipient must have a **Florida driver’s license or vehicle registration.**

**Do customers need proof of voucher purchase?**

✅ **No.** Vouchers are <strong>electronically attached to driver records</strong> in Florida’s registration system.
✅ Customers can redeem their voucher at <strong>any tax collector’s office</strong> without a receipt.

## Personalized Plates & Vehicle Eligibility

**How do personalized plates work?**

✅ Personalized plates are available through any tax collector’s office.
✅ **FSWI is a "side design" plate and limited to five characters.**
📩 **For personalized plate inquiries:** ectc@EscambiaTaxCollector.com

**Which vehicles are eligible for a Florida Stands with Israel specialty plate?**

✅ **Eligible Vehicles:**

- Automobiles
- Leased cars
- Light trucks
- Small/heavy trucks (under 7,999 GVW)
- Trailers, utility trailers
- RVs & motorhomes

❌ **Not Eligible:**

- Vehicles registered under the **International Registration Plan**
- Commercial trucks requiring **two license plates**
- Truck tractors

## Motorcycles & Business Orders

**Can vouchers be purchased for motorcycles?**

❌ **Not at this time.**
🚀 **FSWI is working to introduce a motorcycle-sized plate.**

**Can businesses buy multiple plates for their fleet?**

✅ **Yes.** Businesses can purchase a voucher <strong>for each vehicle or trailer.</strong>
💡 **Tax Deduction:** Depending on tax status, businesses <strong>may be able to deduct all or part of the fees.</strong>
